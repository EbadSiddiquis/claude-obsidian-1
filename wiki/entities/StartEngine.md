# StartEngine

**StartEngine** is the second-largest [[Regulation Crowdfunding|Reg CF]] [[Funding Portal|funding portal]] by dollars raised, offering the most complete vertical stack among the major platforms. (Source: [[Angel Investors Network - Reg CF Platform Comparison]])

---

- **2025 Reg CF raised:** ~$89 million, roughly **24%** share (second to Wefunder). (Source: [[Angel Investors Network - Reg CF Platform Comparison]])
- **Stack:** primary offerings + secondary trading + accredited-investor fund access (most "full-service" of the three).
- **Investor base:** mixed, between Wefunder's retail tilt and Republic's accredited tilt.
