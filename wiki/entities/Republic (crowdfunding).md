# Republic (crowdfunding)

**Republic** is a major [[Regulation Crowdfunding|Reg CF]] platform positioned toward a more curated, accredited-leaning investor base and broader private-markets exposure. (Source: [[Angel Investors Network - Reg CF Platform Comparison]])

---

- **2025 Reg CF raised:** ~$20 million (fourth among Reg CF platforms by this measure). (Source: [[Angel Investors Network - Reg CF Platform Comparison]])
- **Positioning:** tightest acceptance filter, most sophisticated investor base, path toward multi-asset private markets.
- **Investor base:** more accredited (est. 50-60%) due to institutional positioning.

> [!note] Republic operates across multiple offering types beyond Reg CF (e.g., Reg A+, Reg D, private deals), so its Reg-CF-only ranking understates its total platform scale.

## Confidence

**medium** — single 2025 market-analysis source; Reg-CF-only figure is narrower than Republic's full activity.
