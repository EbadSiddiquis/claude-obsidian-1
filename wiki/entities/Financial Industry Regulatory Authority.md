# Financial Industry Regulatory Authority

**FINRA** is the self-regulatory organization (SRO) that oversees U.S. broker-dealers and [[Funding Portal|funding portals]], under SEC supervision. Reg CF intermediaries must be FINRA members. (Source: [[FINRA - Funding Portals]])

---

## Role in crowdfunding

- Administers the **Funding Portal Rules** and the membership-application process for new portals. (Source: [[FINRA - Funding Portals]])
- Maintains the **Funding Portal Registration Depository (FPRD)** and a public list of regulated portals.
- Collects annual **Form FP-Statement of Revenue** filings from portals.
- Handles examinations, investigations, and arbitration for member portals.

## Relationship to the SEC

FINRA is overseen by the [[U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission]]. Broker-dealers register with the SEC on Form BD via FINRA's CRD system; funding portals register on Form Funding Portal via [[EDGAR]] and then join FINRA. (Source: [[FINRA - Funding Portals]])
