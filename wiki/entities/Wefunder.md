# Wefunder

**Wefunder** is the largest [[Regulation Crowdfunding|Reg CF]] [[Funding Portal|funding portal]] by dollars raised. It launched in **2011**, before the JOBS Act passed, and positions itself as "home of the Community Round." (Source: [[Angel Investors Network - Reg CF Platform Comparison]])

---

- **2025 Reg CF raised:** ~$109 million, roughly **33%** of all Reg CF dollars (market leader). (Source: [[Angel Investors Network - Reg CF Platform Comparison]])
- **Investor base:** skews heavily retail / non-accredited (est. 70-80%).
- **Positioning:** broadest deal flow, highest volume, community-driven discovery.

## Confidence

**medium** — raise figures and share come from a single 2025 market-analysis source; treat as directional, cross-check against SEC Reg CF data.
