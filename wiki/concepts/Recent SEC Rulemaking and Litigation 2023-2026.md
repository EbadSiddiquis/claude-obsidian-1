# Recent SEC Rulemaking and Litigation 2023-2026

A roundup of the major SEC disclosure/market-structure rules of 2023-2026 and their sharply divergent fates. The throughline: ambitious disclosure mandates ran into **Administrative Procedure Act (APA)** challenges and a changed Commission, while market-plumbing rules stuck. (Source: [[Orrick - Fifth Circuit Vacates Share Repurchase Rules]])

## The buyback vacatur

The share-repurchase rule (May 3, 2023) would have required detailed daily-repurchase tables. On Oct 31, 2023 the Fifth Circuit held the SEC acted **arbitrarily and capriciously** under the APA (inadequate response to comments, deficient cost-benefit analysis) and gave it 30 days to fix. The SEC could not, and the court **vacated** the rule on **Dec 19, 2023**. Companies need not comply. (Source: [[Orrick - Fifth Circuit Vacates Share Repurchase Rules]])

## Pattern

- **Disclosure-expansion rules** (climate, buybacks) drew APA / major-questions challenges and did not survive.
- **Operational / investor-protection rules** (T+1, cyber-incident reporting) took effect and stuck.
