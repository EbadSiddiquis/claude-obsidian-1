# Form C

**Form C** is the offering statement an issuer files on [[EDGAR]] to conduct a [[Regulation Crowdfunding]] raise. It is the Reg CF analogue of a registration statement, scaled for small issuers. (Source: [[SEC.gov - Regulation Crowdfunding]])

---

## The Form C family

- **Form C** — initial offering statement, filed **before** the offering goes live. Discloses the business, financials, use of proceeds, target/maximum amounts, ownership, and risk factors. (Source: [[SEC.gov - Regulation Crowdfunding]])
- **Form C/A** — amendment to a Form C (material changes to the offering).
- **Form C-U** — progress update (e.g., reaching the target amount or closing).
- **Form C-AR** — annual report, due within **120 days** of fiscal year-end; also posted on the issuer's website.
- **Form C-AR/A** — amendment to an annual report.
- **Form C-TR** — termination of reporting notice.

## Financial-statement tiers

The level of financial review required scales with the amount raised (self-certified / reviewed / audited), with first-time issuers raising larger amounts facing review rather than full audit. (Source: [[SEC.gov - Regulation Crowdfunding]])
